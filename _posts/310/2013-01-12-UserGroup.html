--- layout: 310-post title: "User Group Info" date: 2013-12-01 17:00 categories: "310" --- The first ‘virtual’ Unplugged Controls User Group meeting kicks off on Thursday March 6th at 10am EST (3pm GMT).
This meeting is focused on the Open Source Unplugged Mobile Controls Project hosted on OpenNTF.

These meeting are going to be focused on demonstrating one control each session (a deep dive), demonstrating a production app that uses the controls and then Q and A from the group.

For the first meeting the details are:
  • Brief introduction into resources for using the Unplugged Controls (Videos, support etc) – 5-10 mins
  • Deep Dive – The UnpFormEditor Control – 15 to 20 mins
  • Real Life Case Study – Athabasca University, a Study Guide application (reference manual) for Students. – 15-20 mins
  • Q & A – up to 15 mins
